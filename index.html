OUR MANIFESTO

Bridging Haute Couture and Circular Science

At the intersection of fashion's highest expressions and the empirical precision of data analytics lies Orbital Fashion. Founded on the belief that luxury is defined by longevity, our platform provides real-time market intelligence on high-fashion resale velocity, demand indexes, and structural circularity metrics.

A project by Justin Jao and Yogee Samal

Core Methodologies

  • The Heritage Velocity Index (HVI)

    A proprietary weighting algorithm measuring how rapidly a brand's products transition, resell, and appreciate on secondary markets relative to retail prices.

  • Circular Flow Tracking

    Measuring the environmental offset of vintage transactions compared to new item production, visualizing carbon and water preservation indexes.

  • Archival Search Prediction

    Using advanced runway pattern recognition to anticipate which vintage silhouettes and designers will trend following new collection debuts.
